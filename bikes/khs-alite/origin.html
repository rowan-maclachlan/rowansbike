This is the second bike I ever owned. Purchased from Bike Universe in Saskatoon, it was a good little beater for kid riding around town and up and down the little trails along the river valley with his friends. The Alite line had a few variations (300, 500), and this was the lowest tier. It came outfitted with Shimano Tourney grip shifters and drive-train, and an un-servicable front fork, but the frame has always been amenable to upgrades. A new cockpit, new front wheel, and new RockShox Tora front fork are a few of the upgrades this bike has received over the years.
