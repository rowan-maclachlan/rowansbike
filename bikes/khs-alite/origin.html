This is the second bike I ever owned. Purchased from Bike Universe in Saskatoon, it was pretty run of the mill. The Alite line had a few variations (300, 500), and this was the lowest tier. Despite the low-tier stock grip shifters and the un-servicable front fork, the frame has always been amenable to upgrades. A new cockpit, new front wheel, and new RockShox Tora front fork are a few of the upgrades this bike has received over the years.
